Astro Shooter 3000

An Asteroids clone, a classic arcade-style game built using JavaScript, showcasing my skills in programming. In this game, players control a spaceship navigating through an asteroid field, aiming to destroy asteroids while avoiding collisions to survive and score points. This clone captures the essence of the original game, offering smooth controls, dynamic asteroid movement, and engaging gameplay. With polished graphics and intuitive mechanics, it provides players with an enjoyable and nostalgic gaming experience reminiscent of the arcade era.

Tattoo Studio

This frontend project for the tattoo studio aims to create an immersive and user-friendly digital experience for both potential clients and existing customers. Through sleek and intuitive design, we enable users to explore the studio's portfolio, browse artist profiles, and schedule appointments seamlessly. The interface reflects the studio's unique vibe and showcases its artwork, fostering a connection between clients and artists. With responsive design and interactive features, the project enhances the studio's online presence, making it a vibrant hub for tattoo enthusiasts and seekers of artistic expression alike.
